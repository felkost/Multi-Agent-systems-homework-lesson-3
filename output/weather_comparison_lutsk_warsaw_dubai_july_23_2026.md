# Weather Comparison: Lutsk, Warsaw, and Dubai on July 23, 2026

## Research Goal
To compare the weather conditions in Lutsk (Ukraine), Warsaw (Poland), and Dubai (UAE) on July 23, 2026, using reliable sources and typical climate data.

## Available Data and Limitations
- Direct weather forecasts for the exact date July 23, 2026, were not available for any of the cities.
- Typical weather patterns and historical climate data were used to infer expected conditions.
- Real-time and extended forecasts from popular weather services were inaccessible or did not provide specific data for the requested date.

## Typical Weather Patterns in Late July

### Lutsk, Ukraine
- Temperate continental climate.
- Warm temperatures, generally 18-24 °C (mid-60s to mid-70s °F).
- Possible rain and thunderstorms.
- High UV index possible.

### Warsaw, Poland
- Temperate climate with warm summers.
- Temperatures generally 18-29 °C (mid-60s to mid-80s °F).
- Possible thundery showers and gentle breezes.
- Moderate to high UV index.

### Dubai, United Arab Emirates
- Hot desert climate.
- Extremely high temperatures in July, typically ranging from 38-43 °C (100-110 °F).
- Very low chance of rain.
- High UV index, very strong sun exposure.
- Generally clear skies and dry conditions.

## Comparative Summary for July 23, 2026

| Aspect           | Lutsk, Ukraine           | Warsaw, Poland          | Dubai, UAE                |
|------------------|-------------------------|------------------------|---------------------------|
| Temperature      | Warm, 18-24 °C          | Warm, 18-29 °C         | Very hot, 38-43 °C        |
| Precipitation    | Possible rain/thunder   | Possible thundery showers| Very low chance of rain   |
| Wind             | Gentle breeze possible  | Gentle breeze          | Light to moderate winds   |
| UV Index         | High                    | Moderate to high       | Very high                 |
| Weather Risks    | Thunderstorms, rain     | Thunderstorms, rain    | Heat stress, sunburn risk |

## Conclusion
On July 23, 2026, Lutsk and Warsaw are expected to have warm summer weather with some chance of rain and thunderstorms, accompanied by gentle breezes. Dubai, in contrast, will experience extremely hot and dry conditions with very high UV exposure and minimal chance of precipitation. Visitors to Dubai should prepare for intense heat and sun exposure, while those in Lutsk and Warsaw should be ready for variable weather including possible storms.
